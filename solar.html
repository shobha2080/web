Virtual Short Term Power Trading



In the context of solar energy refers to the trading of electricity generated from solar power in the short-term energy markets, typically over periods ranging from minutes to a few hours. It involves the sale or purchase of solar-generated electricity based on real-time demand and supply conditions in the grid.


Solar energy producers (such as solar farms or large installations) participate in electricity markets by selling the power they generate. Due to the intermittent nature of solar power (dependent on sunlight), it is crucial for producers to engage in short-term trading to manage fluctuations in energy production.
