Child & Family Programs

Young Artists in the Garden

Learn about nature through different garden-themed crafts, activities, stories and games! Young Artists in the Garden is held on alternating Wednesdays and Saturday mornings at Weisbrod Learning .

Seedlings

Intended for children ages 3-6, accompanied by caregivers, Seedlings is a great way for young explorers to build meaningful connections with nature. Each class begins with a hands-on activity related to a weekly theme. The group then adventures outside, rain or shine, to explore different Garden habitats. Class ends back in the classroom with a story related to the weekly theme.
